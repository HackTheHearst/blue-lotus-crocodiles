Target Audience:

K-12 students

Why does cultural heritage matter?
How does history relate to our lives?
GOALS

To facilitate exploration, discovery, and engagement with the past

To create an interface that mirrors the process of archaeology; looking at things relationally
To create an experiential learning environment

USE CASE

Designed to be used in a classroom or gallery; the tool is intended to provoke collaborative learning.

DEMO

USER TESTING at Albany Middle School*

* who claimed no knowledge of Ancient Egypt, but actually knew & recognized quite a bit
"I like the layout. If you see something that you're curious what it is, you can just click on. But also it's not just a word description. You actually have a visual."

USER FEEDBACK + CHANGES WE MADE

  • Users wanted confirmation for correct answers; we turned targets green
  • Users wanted rewards, liked mummies; we added a mummmified crocodile image when matching game was completed successfully
  • Users wanted instructions, particularly on gallery page; we added explanatory text
  • Users wanted more games for other cultures and eras, i.e., Ancient Greece, Civil War
